KC-6 is an RC rover with 6 wheels, where front wheels have rocker boggy suspension system. it can be controlled wirelessy by bluthooth. it has 6 individual motors and 3 motor control driver to provide maximum power. it was made for competion in my school. this was my first hardware project. it took me lot of time to understand stuff, and how things work. the chassies is 3d printed or made in cad. i ordered one from amazon, thought the enclosure for the base of chasses was made by me. it is made up of wood and wooden sticks at top attached with magnetics, so u can open it up and repair or use it.
